Modus Operandi

A VR detective game for Vive played using your voice

Instructions:
    Run the exe as administrator
    Click the microphone icon on the widget that appears at the top of the screen

Controls:
    Hold the trigger and use your voice to talk
    [DEBUG] Press space to advance if it doesn't pick up the command
